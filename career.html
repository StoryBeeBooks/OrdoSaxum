üíº Career Opportunities

We're always seeking talented individuals to join our growing team. Whether you're an experienced professional or beginning your career in the stone industry, we offer positions that challenge, reward, and inspire.

Fabrication & Production

  • Stone Fabrication Technician: Execute precision cutting, edging, and finishing operations on natural stone and sintered surfaces using advanced equipment.
  • CNC Machine Operator: Program and operate computer-controlled machinery to produce flawless countertops, backsplashes, and custom installations.
  • Quality Control Specialist: Ensure every piece meets our exacting standards through detailed inspection and quality assurance processes.
  • Production Manager: Oversee fabrication operations, coordinate workflows, and drive continuous improvement initiatives.

Installation & Field Services

  • Installation Specialist: Perform expert installation of countertops, backsplashes, and custom stone features with precision and care.
  • Field Technician: Conduct on-site templating, measurements, and client consultations using digital measurement technology.
  • Installation Team Lead: Manage installation crews, coordinate logistics, and ensure flawless project execution.

Sales & Design

  • Sales & Design Consultant: Guide clients through material selection, provide design expertise, and transform visions into detailed project plans.
  • Showroom Manager: Curate our material displays, assist customers, and create an inspiring environment for material selection.
  • Business Development Manager: Build relationships with designers, architects, and contractors to expand our reach and impact.

Project Management & Operations

  • Project Manager: Oversee projects from initial consultation through final installation, ensuring seamless execution and client satisfaction.
  • Operations Coordinator: Manage scheduling, logistics, and workflow optimization across all departments.
  • Inventory & Materials Manager: Oversee material sourcing, inventory management, and supplier relationships.

  • Portfolio or work samples (if applicable to your position)
  • Professional references

Note: Even if you don't see a specific opening that matches your expertise, we encourage you to reach out. We're always interested in connecting with exceptional talent.
