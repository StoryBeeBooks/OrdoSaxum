OrdoSaxum is a premier provider of natural stone, sintered slabs, and expert fabrication services. Our name combines "Ordo" (Latin for order, arrangement) and "Saxum" (Latin for stone), reflecting our commitment to bringing perfect order and beauty to stone craftsmanship.

We serve design professionals, architects, contractors, and homeowners across Canada, delivering exceptional quality stone solutions for residential and commercial projects. From luxury kitchens and bathrooms to stunning fireplaces and custom installations, we transform spaces with the timeless elegance of stone.

‚ùì How To's

How to Choose the Right Stone

  1. Consider the application (kitchen, bathroom, fireplace, etc.)
  2. Evaluate durability requirements and maintenance preferences
  3. Review color and pattern options that match your design vision
  4. Understand the characteristics of natural vs. sintered stone
  5. Consult with our design experts for personalized recommendations

How to Care for Your Stone Surfaces

  • Clean daily with pH-neutral stone cleaner and soft cloth
  • Wipe up spills immediately to prevent staining
  • Use cutting boards and trivets to protect surfaces
  • Reseal natural stone annually (sintered stone requires no sealing)
  • Avoid harsh chemicals, acids, and abrasive cleaners
