The OrdoSaxum Sintered Collection

Sintered stone represents the pinnacle of surface technology, offering extreme resistance to scratches, heat, and fire. Forged from natural, recyclable minerals, its completely non-porous structure makes it impossible to stain, inherently hygienic, and maintenance-free, as it never requires sealing. This invulnerability extends to the outdoors, where its UV-resistant properties guarantee color stability for weatherproof applications like facades and outdoor kitchens. Its availability in large, lightweight formats provides unparalleled design versatility, making it a superior, eco-friendly choice for everything from countertops and flooring to large-scale wall cladding.
