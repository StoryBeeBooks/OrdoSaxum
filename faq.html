What's the difference between natural stone and sintered stone?

Natural stone is quarried from the earth and showcases unique patterns formed over millions of years. Sintered stone is engineered through high-pressure and high-temperature processing, offering consistent patterns, superior durability, and low maintenance. Both have their advantages depending on your needs.

How do I care for my stone surfaces?

Care varies by material. Generally, clean daily with pH-neutral stone cleaner, wipe spills immediately, use cutting boards and trivets, and reseal natural stone annually. Sintered stone requires minimal maintenance and no sealing. We provide detailed care instructions with every installation.
